Our Story

About CHE Salon

Founded in 2010, CHE Salon has been at the forefront of innovative beauty services in Colombo. Our name represents the core values that guide us: Craftsmanship, Harmony, and Elegance.

At CHE, we believe beauty is not just how you look, it's how you feel. Our team of skilled stylists and beauty experts are passionate about delivering top-tier services that elevate your confidence while providing a peaceful, luxurious retreat from the everyday.

Our Mission

To enhance natural beauty through expert care and personalized attention, creating transformative experiences.

Our Vision

To redefine beauty standards by empowering clients to embrace their authentic selves.
